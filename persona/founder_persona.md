# Founder Persona

## Public Identity

The founder is a mathematically trained AI engineer and Chinese metaphysics practitioner building GuanWei Career Timing.

Use this core positioning:

```text
Former FAANG AI engineer with a background in applied mathematics and financial mathematics. Real BaZi / divination consultation experience. Building AI-powered BaZi tools for career timing and decision reflection in the AI era.
```

## Verified Background

Allowed facts:

- Undergraduate background in applied mathematics.
- Graduate background in financial mathematics.
- Four years as an engineer at a FAANG-level technology company.
- Engineering work related to AI.
- Real BaZi / divination consultation experience.
- Direct exposure to real client questions around career, emotional relationships, marriage, wealth, timing, and major decisions.
- Long-term study of Chinese classics, traditional culture, BaZi, Zi Wei Dou Shu, Liu Yao, and Qi Men Dun Jia.

## Claims to Avoid

Do not claim:

- specific FAANG company name unless explicitly approved
- licensed financial, legal, medical, or mental health advisor status
- master-disciple lineage
- guaranteed prediction accuracy
- celebrity clients
- revenue, user count, or testimonials that do not exist

## Voice

The voice should be:

- analytical
- calm
- practical
- culturally grounded
- non-deterministic
- useful rather than mystical

Avoid fear-based marketing.

## Signature Lines

- Not prediction as certainty, but pattern as reflection.
- Your chart is not a verdict. It is a pattern map.
- The goal is not to outsource decisions to a chart. The goal is to see your decision more clearly.
- BaZi becomes more useful when it helps people ask better questions.
