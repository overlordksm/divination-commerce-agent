## Website Strategy

Create a focused site or microsite for the career line.

The current `guanweibazi.com` can remain the broader BaZi product base. The new site should handle positioning, conversion, Stripe checkout, and the first focused offer.

Recommended first site map:

- `/`: AI Era Career Timing landing page.
- `/archetype`: free AI-era career archetype test.
- `/report`: paid report sales page.
- `/about`: founder story.
- `/privacy`: privacy policy.
- `/terms`: terms and disclaimer.

## First Offer

Primary name:

```text
AI Era Career Timing Report
```

Consumer hook:

```text
Will AI amplify you or expose you? A BaZi career timing report for the AI era.
```

Pricing ladder:

- `$9`: mini report.
- `$39`: deep report.
- `$99-$149`: human-reviewed report.

Avoid:

- "AI will replace you."
- "Your fate is fixed."
- "Guaranteed career prediction."
- "Certain wealth destiny."

Prefer:

- pattern map
- timing windows
- reflective guidance
- better career questions
- strengths and blind spots
