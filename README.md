# Divination Commerce Agent

This repository contains the operating system for a business opportunity agent.

The agent's long-term role is not limited to one product idea. Its job is to continuously scan market signals, translate demand into product hypotheses, design monetization paths, test distribution channels, and learn from revenue data.

## Agent Name

Working name:

**SignalForge**

Chinese name:

**信号锻造**

Definition:

> SignalForge is a business opportunity agent that continuously scans market signals, translates customer anxiety and demand into product hypotheses, designs monetization paths, tests distribution channels, and learns from revenue data.

Current first experiment:

> GuanWei Career Timing: BaZi career timing for the AI era.

## Current Focus

The first commercial experiment is focused on:

```text
AI-era career decision-making x BaZi / Chinese metaphysics x founder-led distribution
```

The goal is to validate whether AI-era career anxiety can be converted into:

- social content engagement
- email capture
- free archetype test completion
- paid report clicks
- Stripe purchases

## Repository Structure

- `agent.md`: long-term agent identity, rules, and operating modules.
- `brand_strategy.md`: naming, positioning, website, and offer strategy for the current GuanWei Career Timing experiment.
- `ops_setup.md`: required founder inputs and recurring operating workflows.
- `platform_profiles.md`: LinkedIn, X, and Reddit profile assets optimized for trust and interaction.
- `persona/founder_persona.md`: founder background, public claims, and allowed wording.
- `prompts/`: reusable prompts for trend scanning, productization, distribution, experiments, and review.
- `schemas/`: structured output schemas.
- `experiments/guanwei-career-timing/`: first experiment assets.
- `run_agent.py`: no-dependency local prototype for generating first-pass opportunity cards.

## Core Loop

```text
market signal
-> customer anxiety
-> product hypothesis
-> paid offer
-> distribution test
-> revenue signal
-> learning update
```

## Safety Rules

- Do not make deterministic claims about fate, career outcomes, health, wealth, relationships, or legal decisions.
- Do not use fear-based manipulation.
- Do not fake testimonials, client volume, credentials, or revenue.
- Treat BaZi and Chinese metaphysics as reflective frameworks, not guaranteed prediction engines.
- High-risk actions such as posting, commenting, DMing, and pricing changes require founder review.
