# GuanWei Career Timing Experiment

## Hypothesis

People facing AI-era career uncertainty will engage with and pay for a BaZi-based career timing product if the positioning is credible, non-deterministic, and tied to practical career questions.

## Offer

Name:

```text
AI Era Career Timing Report
```

Free entry:

```text
AI Era BaZi Career Archetype
```

Paid ladder:

- `$9`: mini report.
- `$39`: deep report.
- `$99-$149`: human-reviewed report.

## Target User

English-speaking professionals who are concerned about:

- AI replacing or changing their work.
- choosing a new skill direction.
- career transition timing.
- whether to stay, move, build, or wait.
- how to understand their work pattern under uncertainty.

## Core Message

```text
Will AI amplify you or expose you?
Use BaZi as a pattern map for career timing, strengths, blind spots, and better decisions in the AI era.
```

## Seven-Day Test

Day 1:

- publish founder intro
- publish landing page
- verify Stripe payment links

Day 2:

- post LinkedIn founder story
- publish 3 X posts

Day 3:

- post one Reddit value-first question
- collect user language

Day 4:

- revise landing page based on objections
- create one archetype thread

Day 5:

- publish one career archetype post
- ask for 10 beta readers

Day 6:

- review analytics and Stripe clicks
- improve CTA

Day 7:

- decide continue / revise / stop

## Success Metrics

- landing page visit to test start: > 25%
- test start to completion: > 35%
- completion to email capture: > 10%
- completion to paid click: > 5%
- purchase conversion: > 1%
